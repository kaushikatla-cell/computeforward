// About

CS education is broken.
We're fixing it.

Most students learn to code from disjointed tutorials with no structure, no rigor, and no community. ComputeForward is a structured pipeline that turns curious beginners into competitive programmers.

⚡

Competition-Grade Rigor

Our curriculum is reverse-engineered from USACO Bronze through Gold. Every concept maps to real competition problems, not toy exercises.

🧬

Cohort-Based Learning

Students progress in small cohorts with peer accountability, live workshops, and 1:1 mentorship from advanced competitive programmers.

🎯

Measurable Outcomes

We track USACO promotions, problems solved, and project completions. Our students don't just learn—they demonstrate results.

🌍

Free & Accessible

No paywalls. No gatekeeping. Our mission is to make elite CS education available to every motivated student regardless of background.

// Programs

Three levels. One pipeline.

Structured progression from fundamentals to competition-level problem solving. Each level builds directly on the last.

Level 1

Python Foundations

8 weeks · Beginner

Master Python from scratch with a focus on computational thinking. Build real projects, not just scripts. Designed for students with zero prior experience.

  • Variables & Types
  • Control Flow
  • Functions
  • File I/O
  • OOP Basics
  • Mini Projects
Level 2

Data Structures

8 weeks · Intermediate

Internalize the data structures that power every algorithm. Implement each from scratch and apply them to real problems from USACO Bronze.

  • Arrays & Strings
  • Linked Lists
  • Stacks & Queues
  • Hash Maps
  • Trees & Graphs
  • USACO Bronze
Level 3

Algorithms & Competition

10 weeks · Advanced

Deep dive into algorithmic patterns, optimization, and competitive programming strategy. Target: USACO Silver and beyond.

  • Sorting & Searching
  • Greedy Algorithms
  • Dynamic Programming
  • Graph Algorithms
  • Binary Search
  • USACO Silver+
